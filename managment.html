The School Management

The engine room of our school is the School Administration, a formidable pentagon spearheaded by the decisive leadership of Principal Mr. Charged with the vital task of day-to-day management, they are the driving force behind a seamless and enriching educational journey for our students.
They include:

The Board Of Management

Established under the Basic Education Act of 2013, the Board of Management (BOM) holds the primary responsibility for the management and development of the school.

The Parent`s Association

The Parent's Association plays a crucial role in ensuring the general welfare of both the staff and the students within the school.

The School Administration

Led by the Principal, Mr. D. Chomba, the School Administration forms the core operational team responsible for the day-to-day running of the school. This dedicated group ensures the smooth functioning of all school activities and strives to provide the best possible experience for all students.
